Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How can I place an order for products?

You can place an order by calling our sales team at +233 20 181 8195 or by sending an email to sales@konacarelimited.com. Our representatives will guide you through the ordering process.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services across Ghana. Delivery times and fees may vary depending on your location and the size of your order. Contact us for specific details.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money, and cash. For large orders, we may require a deposit before processing.

How can I get technical support for medical equipment?

For technical support, please contact our service department at +233 54 152 1002 or email info@konacarelimited.com. We provide maintenance and repair services for all equipment we supply.
