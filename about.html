Our Story

Committed to excellence in healthcare since our inception

Leading Healthcare Solutions Provider in Ghana

Konacare Ghana Limited was established in May 2023 as a premier supplier of medical equipment, medical devices, and comprehensive healthcare products in Ghana. We have rapidly established ourselves as a trusted partner for hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and healthcare providers across the country.

Our Business Activities

Our major business activities include:

  • Supply of Medical equipment
  • Supply of Medical devices
  • Supply of Medical Disposables (consumables)
  • Cosmetics
  • Food products
  • Wholesale and Retail of Herbal medicine and herbal products
  • Importation of Goods and General merchandise
  • Other health related services

At Konacare, we are committed to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to understanding your needs and providing tailored solutions that meet your specific requirements.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our operations

Our Vision

To be Ghana's most trusted partner in advancing healthcare delivery by providing innovative medical solutions, high-quality products, and sustainable partnerships that improve lives and communities.

Our Mission

We exist to make healthcare accessible, reliable, and affordable by supplying quality medical equipment, devices, consumables, food, cosmetics, and herbal products. Through strategic partnerships and revenue-sharing models, we empower hospitals and health facilities to deliver better care while ensuring long-term value for stakeholders.
